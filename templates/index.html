THRUSANG'23 is a two-day national festival with the subject TECHEKYAM. The fest had to live up to its name and ethos, which meant that all the events in this two-day event had to be equally distributed. The activities of this technical event are mostly centered on technical workshops and other activities. The activities ranged from fundamental and easy Arduino instruction for school children to mastering ethical hacking. It has grown into a Science and Technology Festival, attracting people from all walks of life, including students, academia, professionals, and the general public. So, call your friends, mark your calendars, and gear up for Thrusang’23: TECHEKYAM!
