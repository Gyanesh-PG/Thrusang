About Us


K L UNIVERSITY

The Koneru Lakshmaiah Charities was established as a trust in 1980 with its official address at Museum Road, Governorpet, Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh 520 002 and started KL College of Engineering in the Academic year 1980-81. KL Deemed to be University was established in 1980-81, as KL College of Engineering, which was upgraded to KL College of Engineering Autonomous in 2006 by UGC and was declared as a Deemed to be University in 2009 by UGC, MHRD Govt.of India. In 2012 as a Deemed to be University the institution was accredited by NAAC with A Grade and later in 2018, was re-accredited by NAAC with A++ grade. In 2019 UGC, MHRD declared this institution as Category I Institution.

THRUSANG

THRUSANG is the result of the first generation of Electronics and Computer Engineering students' hard work. It was the inaugural branch festival, which sparked a new chapter in the college's history. THRUSANG has pleasantly surprised its audience since its inception. It was dubbed Mini-Samyak, a moniker that belies the event's incredible magnitude. Thrusang strives to strengthen all parts of a student's abilities outside of the classroom. The students that participated learnt a variety of life skills and are now operating as executives in major multinational companies.

ESSENCE-ECM

In the year 2012, the Elegant Student Synergy of Electronics and Computer Engineers (ESSENCE) was established. ESSENCE was established with the goal of bridging the gap between teachers and students outside of the classroom. ESSENCE focuses on the development of an individual from the Department of ECM by integrating students in several wings to enhance their technical or non-technical abilities under the supervision of the faculty. ESSENCE oversees organizing events such as T-Day (Teachers Day Celebrations), Thrusang (ECM Dept. Techno Management Fest, also known as Mini Samyak), several workshops and conferences, and social activities. Apart from classroom learning, ESSENCE attempts to improve all aspects of a student's talents.
